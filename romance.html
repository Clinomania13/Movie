LA LA LAND

2016 • Romance

STARRING

  • Ryan Gosling as Sebastian Wilder
  • Emma Stone as Mia Dolan
  • John Legend as Keith

An aspiring actress working in a coffee shop and a passionate jazz pianist struggling to open his own club fall deeply in love while pursuing their artistic dreams in the magical yet challenging landscape of Los Angeles. Damien Chazelle's enchanting modern musical masterpiece swept the awards season with a record-tying 14 Oscar nominations, ultimately winning six including Best Director and Best Actress. The film's stunning cinematography, memorable original songs by Justin Hurwitz, and bittersweet ending explore the painful tension between romantic love and personal ambition with breathtaking dance sequences and emotional depth. Emma Stone won the Academy Award for Best Actress for her luminous and vulnerable performance that captures the hope and heartbreak of chasing Hollywood dreams.

THE NOTEBOOK

2004 • Romance

STARRING

  • Ryan Gosling as Noah Calhoun
  • Rachel McAdams as Allie Hamilton
  • James Garner as Duke / Older Noah

A poor yet passionate young man falls intensely in love with a rich young woman from a different social class during one magical summer in 1940s South Carolina, giving her a profound sense of freedom and true love despite family opposition and societal expectations. Based on Nicholas Sparks' bestselling novel, this sweeping epic love story spanning multiple decades became a cultural phenomenon and modern romance classic. The film's iconic rain-soaked kiss scene and the heartbreaking framing story of elderly lovers have made it one of the most beloved romantic films of all time.

BEFORE SUNRISE

1995 • Romance

STARRING

  • Ethan Hawke as Jesse Wallace
  • Julie Delpy as Céline
  • Andrea Eckert as Wife on Train

Two young strangers, an American man and a French woman, meet by chance on a train to Vienna and decide to spend one magical, conversation-filled night together exploring the romantic city before parting ways at sunrise, possibly forever. Richard Linklater's intimate and dialogue-driven romance perfectly captures the beauty of fleeting connection, intellectual chemistry, and the possibility of love at first sight. The film spawned two critically acclaimed sequels that follow the same characters across decades, creating one of cinema's most beloved romantic trilogies.

PRIDE AND PREJUDICE

2005 • Romance

2005 • Romance

STARRING

  • Keira Knightley as Elizabeth Bennet
  • Matthew Macfadyen as Mr. Fitzwilliam Darcy
  • Rosamund Pike as Jane Bennet

In Georgian era England, sparks fly when the spirited and independent Elizabeth Bennet meets the wealthy, seemingly arrogant Mr. Darcy, leading to a romance built on overcoming first impressions, personal growth, and mutual respect. Joe Wright's visually stunning adaptation of Jane Austen's beloved novel breathes vibrant new life into the timeless classic romance with lush cinematography and authentic period detail. Keira Knightley's spirited and nuanced performance earned her an Academy Award nomination while capturing Elizabeth's wit, intelligence, and romantic journey.

CRAZY RICH ASIANS

2018 • Romance / Comedy

STARRING

  • Constance Wu as Rachel Chu
  • Henry Golding as Nick Young
  • Michelle Yeoh as Eleanor Young

A New York economics professor travels to Singapore to meet her boyfriend's extremely wealthy and traditional family for the first time, only to face intense scrutiny and social pressure from his disapproving mother and Singapore's elite society. This groundbreaking romantic comedy became a cultural phenomenon as the first Hollywood studio movie in 25 years to feature a predominantly Asian cast in contemporary roles. The lavish visuals, heartfelt story, and exploration of cultural identity resonated globally while proving the universal appeal and commercial viability of diverse storytelling in mainstream cinema.

BEAUTY AND THE BEAST

2017 • Romance / Fantasy

STARRING

  • Emma Watson as Belle
  • Dan Stevens as the Beast
  • Luke Evans as Gaston

A kind-hearted young woman named Belle is taken prisoner by a fearsome beast in his enchanted castle. Despite her fears, she befriends the castle’s magical staff and learns to look beyond the Beast’s frightening exterior, discovering the kind heart and soul of the true prince within. This live-action adaptation of Disney’s animated classic celebrates love, compassion, and the power of seeing beyond appearances.

STARRING

STARRING

  • Kate Hudson as Andie Anderson
  • Matthew McConaughey as Benjamin Barry
