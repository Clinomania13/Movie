THE SHINING

1980

STARRING

  • Jack Nicholson as Jack Torrance
  • Shelley Duvall as Wendy Torrance
  • Danny Lloyd as Danny Torrance

A struggling writer and recovering alcoholic accepts a position as the winter caretaker of the isolated Overlook Hotel in the Colorado Rockies with his family, where supernatural forces and his own deteriorating mental state slowly drive him toward violent madness and murderous rage. Stanley Kubrick's meticulous adaptation of Stephen King's novel stands as a masterclass in atmospheric horror, psychological tension, and cinematic composition that has influenced generations of filmmakers. Jack Nicholson's increasingly unhinged performance combined with Kubrick's innovative use of Steadicam, symmetrical framing, and unsettling imagery created some of horror cinema's most iconic and enduring moments. The Overlook Hotel itself becomes a malevolent character through the director's haunting visual storytelling and oppressive atmosphere.

THE CONJURING

2013

STARRING

  • Patrick Wilson as Ed Warren
  • Vera Farmiga as Lorraine Warren
  • Lili Taylor as Carolyn Perron

Renowned paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren are called upon to help a family living in a secluded Rhode Island farmhouse who are being terrorized by a dark and malevolent supernatural presence with a horrific history. James Wan crafted a modern horror classic that masterfully combines classic haunted house tropes with innovative scares and genuine character development. The film's critical and commercial success revitalized the supernatural genre and spawned The Conjuring Universe, one of the most successful horror franchises in cinema history.
